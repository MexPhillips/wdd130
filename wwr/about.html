|MEX| RAFTING COMPANY

At |MEX| Rafting Company, our purpose is to connect people with nature through exhilarating river adventures. We are committed to providing safe, high-quality rafting experiences that inspire confidence, teamwork, and a deep appreciation for the great outdoors. Our mission is to guide every guest on a journey that challenges the body, refreshes the mind, and strengthens the spirit. We believe in respecting the river, preserving the environment, and creating unforgettable moments on the water. Our motto: "Adventure with purpose, paddle with heart."

History

Founded in 1985 by two passionate river guides, MEX Rafting Company began as a small operation with a big dream: to share the beauty and thrill of whitewater with everyone. Our founders, Mike and Elena, built the company on a foundation of safety, respect for nature, and a commitment to creating life-long memories. What started with a single raft and a handful of friends has grown into a leading adventure company, but our core values remain the same. We still believe that a day on the river is more than just a trip—it's an adventure that can change your perspective.

Our mission is to provide an escape from the everyday, offering a chance to connect with the raw power of nature and the camaraderie of fellow adventurers. We have carefully selected our river destinations to offer a variety of experiences, from gentle family floats to challenging Class V rapids. Every trip is guided by our team of certified professionals who are not only experts in river safety but also passionate storytellers eager to share their knowledge of the local ecology and history. Join us and discover your next great adventure!
